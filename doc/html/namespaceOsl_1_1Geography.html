Convert geodetic coordinates from decimal degrees to Degrees-Minutes-Seconds (DMS).

This function allows to convert geodetic coordinates from decimal degrees to Degrees-Minutes-Seconds (DMS).

Decimal degrees \(dd\) are converted to DMS coordinates \(d\), \(m\), \(s\) following the algorithm:

  1. \(d = \big\lfloor\left\vert dd\right\vert\big\rfloor\)
  2. \(m_d = 60\left(\left\vert dd\right\vert-d\right)\)
  3. \(m = \big\lfloor m_t\big\rfloor\)
  4. \(s = 60\left(m_t-m\right)\)
  5. \(d = \mathrm{sign}(dd)d\)
Parameters
[in]ddThe decimal degrees representation of the geodetic coordinate.
[out]d,m,sThe degrees, minutes and seconds representation of the geodetic coordinates in that order.

Convert geodetic coordinates from Degrees-Minutes-Seconds (DMS) to decimal degrees.

This function allows to convert geodetic coordinates from Degrees-Minutes-Seconds (DMS) to decimal degrees. The orientation of the coordinates (North-South, or West-East) are taken into account through the sign of the d (degree) parameter with +1 for North or East orientation and -1 for South or West orientation.

DMS coordinates ( \(d\), \(m\), \(s\)) are converted to decimal degrees \(dd\) through the relationship:

\[ dd=\mathrm{sign}(d)\left(\vert d\vert+\dfrac{m}{60}+\dfrac{s}{3600}\right) \]

Parameters
[in]d,m,sThe degrees, minutes and seconds representation of the geodetic coordinates in that order.
Returns
The decimal degrees representation of the geodetic coordinate.
