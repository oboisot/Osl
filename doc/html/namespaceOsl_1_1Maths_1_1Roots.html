Function to find roots of a third order (cubic) polynomial with complex coefficients.

This function finds the roots of a third order polynomial of the form:

\[ f(x)=ax^3+bx^2+cx+d \]

with \(a\), \(b\), \(c\) and \(d\) complex coefficients using Cardano's formula.

Parameters
[in]a,b,c,dthe polynomial coefficients starting from its higher order.
[out]x1,x2,x3the three roots of the quadratic polynomial.
Note
The returned first root x1 is ensured to be real.
