\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class to construct a piecewise Linear Spline interpolator of a 3D vector. 

\DoxyHorRuler{0}
 \doxyparagraph*{Principle}

Knowing vectors coordinates at a given time $t$, this class defines a Linear Spline interpolator for each x, y and z coordinates of the vectors.

Let a set of temporal points $(t_k)_{k\in[\vert0;N\vert]}$ of size $N+1$ being in a strictly increasing order\+:

\[ t_0<t_1<\cdots<t_{n-1}<t_n \]

with their corresponding coordinates $(x_k, y_k, z_k)_{k\in[\vert0;N\vert]}$.

The piecewise Linear Spline interpolation functions at index $k$ for coordinates x, y and z are defined by the first order polynomials\+:

\[ \begin{split} f_{x,k}(t) & =a_{x,k}(t-t_k)+b_{x,k} \\ f_{y,k}(t) & =a_{y,k}(t-t_k)+b_{y,k} \\ f_{z,k}(t) & =a_{z,k}(t-t_k)+b_{z,k} \end{split} ,\quad\forall t\in[t_k; t_{k+1}[\quad ; \quad\forall k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert] \]

providing a set of $3\times N$ interpolation functions with their corresponding coefficients\+:

\[ \begin{split} (f_{x,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} & \mbox{ with } (a_{x,k}, b_{x,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} \\ (f_{y,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} & \mbox{ with } (a_{y,k}, b_{y,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} \\ (f_{z,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} & \mbox{ with } (a_{z,k}, b_{z,k})_{k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert]} \\ \end{split} \]

\doxyparagraph*{Computation of the interpolator coefficients}

Coefficients are determined by ensuring continuity of the interpolation functions (coordinates) at each interval nodes, resulting in the sytem of equations for the $x$ coordinate\+:

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} f_{x,k}(t_k) & = & x_k\\ f_{x,k}(t_{k+1}) & = & x_{k+1}\\ \end{array} \]

Solving this system results in the evaluation of the coefficients for the piecewise cubic interpolation functions $f_{x,k}$\+:

\[ \left\{\begin{array}{rcl} a_{x,k} & = & \dfrac{x_{k+1}-x_{k}}{t_{k+1}-t_k} \\ b_{x,k} & = & x_k \end{array}\right., \quad\forall k\in[\vert0;N-1\vert] \]

The same procedure is applied for y and z coordinates.
